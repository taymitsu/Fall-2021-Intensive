Roots & Fur

Who is R&F?

At Roots & Fur, we believe in the importance of nurturing your roots. Building a supportive environment to encourage growth and love is one of our biggest joys in life.

We have partnered with Humane Society of Silicon Valley to support animals in their care and give them the best shot at finding their forever home. 30% of each purchase is donated to the HSSV.

The inflation of houseplants has increased during the pandemic due to a spike in interest to decorate your home. Many are outraged at unrealistic prices now viewed as “normal” to many of the plant community newcomers. We are working to set a normal standard for plant costs, while still giving back to animals in need. Shop with a purpose and no buyer’s remorse.
